Welcome to Epicobook, a database for Epicodus students.

Test your memory! See whose names and faces you can match together.

This is a two player game where a player will turn over two tiles at the time to try and match a student name with their face. Successful matches will award the player a point, otherwise it will switch to the next player's turn. Once all tiles are matched, the player with the highest number of points wins!
